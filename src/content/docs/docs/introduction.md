## What is Session Buddy?

Session Buddy is a powerful browser extension designed to help you organize, manage, and restore your browser tabs and sessions. Whether you're a power user managing dozens of tabs or someone who wants to keep their workspace organized, Session Buddy makes it easy to save, organize, and access your browsing sessions across your devices.

## Key Features

- **Save Sessions**: Capture your current browser tabs into organized collections for later access
- **Manage Collections**: Organize your saved sessions into custom collections with ease
- **Quick Access**: Instantly open collections or individual links without cluttering your browser
- **Data Protection**: Your data is stored securely with multiple backup options
- **Cross-Device Sync**: Access your sessions from anywhere (with cloud storage enabled)
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find links and collections with powerful search capabilities
