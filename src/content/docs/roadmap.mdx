Updated March, 2026

&nbsp;

Below is the short list of our top priorities. We’ll be updating it as work gets completed.

&nbsp;

- An option to store collections in the cloud for universal access and ultimate data protection
- Chrome tab group support
- Additional view options
  - **Expanded view**: thumbnail, title, URL, and actions &nbsp;<Badge text="v3 regression" variant="note" />
  - **Image view**: page captures, image previews, and video thumbnails
- Undo for collection and link deletes &nbsp;<Badge text="v3 regression" variant="note" />
- Search improvements, including fixes for international characters
- Collection tile ordering (by update date, creation date, or title)
- Drag/drop link reordering
- Improved history management, including event context menus, multi-select, and merging
- A Cut action (in addition to Copy for folders, links, tabs, etc)
- An option to filter duplicates when multiple collections are selected &nbsp;<Badge text="v3 regression" variant="note" />
- The ability to paste a plain list of URLs directly into collections
- The ability to paste clipboard contents directly into the navigation sidebar to create a new collection
- Inclusion of window counts in session and collection headers &nbsp;<Badge text="v3 regression" variant="note" />
- The ability to add descriptions and labels to collections
- Integrations (YouTube, Reddit, ChatGPT, etc)

_… and much more._
