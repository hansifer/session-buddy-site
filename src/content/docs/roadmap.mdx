Below is the short list of our current top priorities. Please [consider a donation](/donate/) to help support our efforts.

<h2></h2>

1. An option to store collections in the cloud for universal access and ultimate data protection
1. Chrome tab group support
1. Additional view options
    1. **Expanded view**: thumbnail, title, URL, and actions <span class="docs-badge">v3 regression</span>
    1. **Image view**: page captures, image previews, and video thumbnails
1. Undo for collection and link deletes <span class="docs-badge">v3 regression</span>
1. Search improvements, including fixes for international characters
1. Collection tile ordering (by update date, creation date, or title)
1. Drag/drop link reordering
1. Improved history management, including event context menus, multi-select, and merging
1. A Cut action (in addition to Copy for folders, links, tabs, etc)
1. An option to filter duplicates when multiple collections are selected <span class="docs-badge">v3 regression</span>
1. The ability to paste a plain list of URLs directly into collections
1. The ability to paste clipboard contents directly into the navigation sidebar to create a new collection
1. Inclusion of window counts in session and collection headers <span class="docs-badge">v3 regression</span>
1. The ability to add descriptions and labels to collections
1. Integrations (YouTube, Reddit, AI chats, etc)

<h2></h2>

_… and much more._
